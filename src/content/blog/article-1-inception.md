## The Foundation

I started my schooling in one of the spirituality-based schools. There, I learned about our history, culture, discipline, and integrity. My teachers inspired me to explore the stories of the Mahabharata and Ramayana. They also introduced me to the Bhagavad Gita.

## Learning the Sacred Verses

I learned how to recite Bhagavad Gita shlokas and understand their insightful meanings. I participated in many Bhagavad Gita shloka competitions and won several awards.

## A Moment of Pride

One of the proudest moments of my childhood was when, at the age of seven, my name was published in a newspaper as "Slokala Rupika." This recognition remains one of the most prestigious moments of my life.

## Beyond Academics

Beyond academics, these experiences shaped my values and curiosity. Up to my 10th standard, I was an above-average student.

These early years laid the foundation for everything that followed. The values I learned, the discipline I developed, and the curiosity that was nurtured during those formative years continue to guide me on my journey today.
