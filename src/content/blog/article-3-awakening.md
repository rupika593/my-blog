## Overcoming Challenges

After Intermediate, I did not secure a good rank in EAPCET. I believed I would not get an engineering seat, but my father encouraged and supported me throughout this difficult period. By God's grace, I received admission to Visakha Institute of Engineering and Technology, where I am currently pursuing my third year of engineering.

## First Year: Discovering Communication and Curiosity

During my first year, I realized the importance of communication skills and exploring new opportunities. I participated in a PowerPoint presentation competition, which sparked my curiosity about India's ancient temples and their mysteries. I began researching temples such as Srirangam, Dwaraka, Padmanabhaswamy, Lepakshi, Chidambaram, and Kamakhya.

I also developed a strong interest in quantum physics and started reading about black holes and the mysteries of the universe.

## Second Year: Embracing Technology and AI

In my second year, I understood the importance of core engineering subjects and emerging technologies. I participated in hackathons and ideathons organized by my college. During this time, I was introduced to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

I learned concepts such as:
- Heuristic search and A* algorithm
- Supervised, unsupervised, and semi-supervised learning
- Decision Trees and Random Forest
- K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN)
- Neural networks and AI agents
- AI tools and applications

This period awakened my passion for both ancient wisdom and cutting-edge technology, showing me that the pursuit of knowledge knows no boundaries.
