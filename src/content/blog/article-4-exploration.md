## My Skill Development Journey

My third year of engineering became one of the biggest turning points in my learning journey.

Dr. Pallam Setti Sir visited our college and motivated us to continuously improve our skills and prepare ourselves for the future of Artificial Intelligence.

Inspired by his guidance, I joined a skill development program.

Shiva Kiran Sir introduced me to Agentic AI and taught me how to build my first portfolio, blogs, and AI projects.

I learned modern tools including Replit, GitHub, Kiro IDE, Antigravity, Cursor, and n8n.

I explored AI models including OpenAI, Gemini, GLM, Ollama, and Sarvam AI.

He also introduced Panchakosha, Indian Knowledge Systems, Bitcoin, Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, Quantum Physics, Richard Feynman's work, and encouraged project-based learning.

This experience transformed my mindset from studying only for examinations to building projects and becoming a lifelong learner.

## Platforms & Websites That Helped Me Learn

### Development Platforms

**Replit**  
Online coding platform for building and testing applications quickly from the browser. It helped me understand project development and deployment.

**GitHub**  
Platform for version control and project collaboration. It helped me manage code, track changes, and publish my projects.

**Kiro IDE**  
AI-powered development environment that helped me understand coding, project structure, debugging, and application development.

**Antigravity**  
AI-assisted development environment that simplified project creation, editing, and rapid experimentation.

**Cursor**  
AI-powered code editor that assists with coding, debugging, and understanding projects faster.

**n8n**  
Workflow automation platform used to build AI agents and automate tasks by connecting different services.

### AI Models & Platforms

**OpenAI**  
AI platform providing powerful language models that helped me learn prompting, AI applications, and intelligent automation.

**Gemini**  
Google's AI model for research, coding, reasoning, and productivity.

**GLM**  
Large Language Model used for AI-based conversations and application development.

**Ollama**  
Platform for running open-source AI models locally on a computer for experimentation and learning.

**Sarvam AI**  
Indian AI platform focused on developing multilingual AI technologies for Indian languages.

### Learning Platforms

**Student Chakra**  
Student learning platform that provides educational resources, opportunities, and skill development.

**Hack2Skill**  
Platform that hosts hackathons, coding competitions, innovation challenges, and skill-building events.

**Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS)**  
Platform for exploring India's traditional knowledge, culture, science, philosophy, mathematics, and heritage.

**STEM IKS**  
Educational platform combining STEM education with Indian Knowledge Systems to encourage interdisciplinary learning.
