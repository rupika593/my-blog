Every meaningful journey begins with curiosity, and mine began with the recitation of Bhagavad Gita shlokas. As I explored the meaning behind each verse, I discovered a world of philosophy, discipline, and timeless wisdom that encouraged me to ask deeper questions about life.

This curiosity naturally led me to explore India's ancient temples. Alongside my school education, I spent my free time learning about their architecture, history, symbolism, engineering, and the civilizations that built them. Every temple became more than a monument—it became a classroom filled with stories, science, culture, and spiritual heritage.

As my perspective expanded, I realized that knowledge is interconnected. A question about history could lead to philosophy, philosophy to psychology, psychology to technology, and technology to innovation. This way of learning shaped my mindset and inspired me to become a lifelong learner.

Today, my dreams continue to evolve. I aspire to build a successful career in technology while exploring psychology, philosophy, astrophysics, archaeology, linguistics, music, sports, and India's cultural heritage. I am also inspired to learn about crime investigation, intelligence studies, and public service. These are aspirations that motivate me to keep learning every day.

For me, this is only the beginning. Every book, every challenge, every conversation, and every new idea is another step in an endless journey of discovery.
